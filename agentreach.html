How it works

Create one agent record, then execute through one ID.

Step 1

Create your agent record.

Call agentreach.agents.create once to create a canonical agent_id for your agent.

Step 2

Provision channel credentials.

Attach phone, email inbox, and virtual card credentials to that same resource.

Step 3

Consume unified events and act.

Read calls, SMS, voicemail, emails, and payment events in one timeline keyed by agent_id.
