The solution

The nervous system for your AI agents — not the brain

Identity

One agent. One permanent address.

Each agent gets a phone number, an email address, and a canonical ID — unified under a single API. State persists across restarts, channels, and time.

Audio passthrough

Raw G.711 µ-law. Zero transcoding.

We stream audio bi-directionally between the PSTN and your model's real-time API. No intermediate TTS/STT hops. Under 10ms internal overhead.

Unified inbox

Every interaction, one timeline.

Calls, voicemails, emails, and system events flow into a single queryable inbox. Your agent polls one endpoint and sees everything.
